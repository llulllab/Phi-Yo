Privacy Policy

Phi-Yo — Last updated: 26 April 2026

1. Who We Are

Phi-Yo is operated by Studio Llull, a research and software production entity based in Paris, France. For data protection purposes, Studio Llull (represented by its founder, Dr Firat Ilim) is the data controller for all personal data processed through this application.

Contact: pariswithflorian@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect and process the following personal data:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account via one-time verification code.
  • Dream entries and journal content — the text you submit for analysis, stored to support your personal reflection history.
  • Session tokens (JWT) — stored locally on your device to maintain your authenticated session.
  • Language and theme preferences — stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect your name, location, health records, or payment information. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

3. Third-Party AI Processing (Anthropic)

Phi-Yo uses Claude, an AI system provided by Anthropic, Inc., to generate dream analysis responses. When you submit a dream entry, your text is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing.

By using Phi-Yo, you acknowledge that your journal content is processed by Anthropic in accordance with Anthropic's Privacy Policy. Anthropic is a subprocessor under our data processing arrangements.

Anthropic may retain submitted content for a limited period as described in their privacy policy. We recommend you do not include highly sensitive personal information (such as names of third parties, health diagnoses, or financial details) in your dream entries.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • To generate and display personalized dream analysis responses.
  • To store your reflection history so you can review past entries.
  • To improve service reliability and diagnose technical issues (server logs, anonymized).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party except as described in this policy (Anthropic for AI processing) or as required by law.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing your email and authentication data to provide the service you have requested.
  • Consent — processing your dream entries through Anthropic's AI system. You provide consent by submitting content through the application.
  • Legitimate interests — server logs and diagnostic data for service reliability.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored on servers located within the European Union (Hetzner, Germany). We do not transfer personal data outside the EEA except to Anthropic (United States) for AI processing as described above. This transfer is covered by Anthropic's standard contractual commitments.

We retain your account data and journal entries for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your account, your data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

Authentication tokens expire automatically after 30 days of inactivity.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for AI processing at any time by discontinuing use of the service.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at pariswithflorian@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority, the CNIL.

8. Children

Phi-Yo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through this service, please contact us for immediate removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Phi-Yo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
